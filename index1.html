Why Cybersecurity Matters

Cybersecurity awareness helps protect your personal data, devices, and online identity from cyber threats such as phishing, malware, and data breaches.

Strong Passwords

Use long, unique passwords and enable multi-factor authentication to secure your accounts.

Safe Browsing

Avoid suspicious links, download software only from trusted sources, and keep your browser updated.
